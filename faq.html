Frequently Asked Questions

Are kids welcome?

While we love kids, we are making this day adult only. Take the night off and get ready for an awesome time!

Shuttle Service?

A shuttle service will be provided; come back later for more information on timelines and pick up locations.

Have dietary restrictions?

Don’t worry, we’re creating a menu that caters to most dietary restrictions and allergies.

Will the wedding be indoors or outdoors?

Both! The ceremony, cocktail hour, and dinner will be held outside, and we will move inside for dancing. Don’t worry, we will have blankets and heaters in case they’re needed for the outdoor portion.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

Nope! We want to see your beautiful face while we walk down the aisle, not the back of your phone. We will give a designated time during the ceremony for you to take a photo, but otherwise, we ask that you refrain from taking photos during the ceremony.
